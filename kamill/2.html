Bisabolol

Bisabolol, an essential oil known for its skin-healing properties. It is one of the most important organic herbs in ancient Western society and is said to have miraculous effects. No only can it cleanse and restore skin cells, it also soothes dry and cracked skin, and increases skin resistance. Another reason why Bisabolol is so widely used in cosmetics products is the fact that it helps other useful ingredients penetrate deeper into the skin.

Lactic Acid

Lactic Acid is an organic acid, the alpha hydroxy acids (AHAs) derived from lactic acid can easily penetrate the top layer of the skin. Thereby enabling the rejuvenation of skin cells to achieve a lighter skin tone; to restore the youthful, soft and delicate skin.

Glycerin

Plant based glycerin effectively improves dry and course skin, and locks in moisture. It acts as an invisible protection layer for the skin.
